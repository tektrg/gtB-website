# BYOM AI Extension: Power-User Setup (No Subscriptions)

Power users don’t need *another* AI subscription. You need **control**:
- pick the model that fits the job
- pay per usage when it’s cheaper
- keep your keys and data flow sane

That’s the point of a **BYOM AI extension** (Bring Your Own Model). Some people call this **BYOK** (Bring Your Own Key). Same goal: stop renting access, start owning the plumbing.
