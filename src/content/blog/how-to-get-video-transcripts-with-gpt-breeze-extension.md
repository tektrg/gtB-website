## Frequently Asked Questions

### **Is GPT Breeze really free to use?**
Yes! The core transcript extraction feature is completely free. There are no subscription fees or hidden charges for accessing video transcripts.

### **Does it work on all YouTube videos?**
GPT Breeze works on any YouTube video that has captions available (either manual or auto-generated). If a video has no captions at all, the feature won't be available for that specific video.

### **Can I use the transcripts for commercial purposes?**
The transcripts belong to the original video content creators. Always respect copyright laws and get proper permissions when using transcripts for commercial purposes.

### **How accurate are the transcripts?**
The accuracy depends on the original caption source. Manual captions are typically 99%+ accurate, while auto-generated captions vary from 80-95% depending on audio quality and speaker clarity.

### **Does GPT Breeze work on other video platforms?**
Currently, the transcript feature is optimized for YouTube, but the extension is continually expanding to support more video platforms.

### **Can I export the transcripts to different formats?**
Yes! GPT Breeze allows you to copy transcripts in plain text format, which can then be pasted into any document processor or saved in your preferred format.

### **Is my data safe when using GPT Breeze?**
Absolutely. GPT Breeze processes transcripts locally in your browser and doesn't store or transmit your video content to external servers.

### **Will this slow down my browser?**
No, GPT Breeze is optimized for performance and only activates when you specifically request a transcript. It has minimal impact on browser speed.
