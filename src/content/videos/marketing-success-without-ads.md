**TL;DR**
Chris created a **wish list app** that has earned him **$150,000 annually** without any team or marketing. He built this app over **six years** while managing a full-time job, finally quitting only two months ago to focus on it completely. To succeed, he emphasized starting with **simple ideas**, engaging with users for **feedback**, and focusing on user satisfaction rather than immediate monetization.
