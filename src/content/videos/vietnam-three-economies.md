**TL;DR**
Vietnam has transformed from **over 60% living below the poverty line 30 years ago** to **under 5% today**, with incomes increasing from **$120 a year to nearly $5,000**. The country operates on **three overlapping economies:** the street economy, the middle economy, and the luxury economy, which together create a diverse socioeconomic environment. The narrative of Vietnam being merely "cheap" overlooks the **rapid growth and modernization** that the nation has achieved, reflecting a significant story of progress since the war.
