## User discussion research

Goal: understand what people look for when replacing Glasp / read-it-later / web highlighter tools, and what pain points an alternative should solve.

Reddit threads:
- https://www.reddit.com/r/zen_browser/comments/1ivf5hf/glasp_alternative/
  - Direct “Glasp alternative” ask; good for scanning replacement criteria.
- https://www.reddit.com/r/ObsidianMD/comments/1hppi69/just_started_using_obsidian_after_years_of_being/
  - Highlights workflow pain: clipping/highlighting, iOS/Safari constraints, wanting “highlights only” export.

Common themes to address in the post:
- People want a **local-first / privacy-first** workflow (avoid vendor lock-in; prefer BYOK).
- “Read it later” products come/go (shutdowns, price increases) → need an exportable, resilient workflow.
- Web highlight + summarization needs to work across: browser pages, PDFs, sometimes YouTube.
- Integration matters: Obsidian/Notion/Markdown exports, and reliable syncing.
